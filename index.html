Vegetable Biryani Recipe

Ingredients of Vegetable Biryani

Basmati rice 400 mg
Large sliced Onion 2
Clove 8
Garlic Paste 2 teaspoon
Chopped vegetables 200 gm
Curd 1/2 cup
Cardamom 4
Water 8 cup
Ghee 2 spoon
cinnamon 2
Bayleaf 2
salt 2 spoon
Garam Masala 4 spoon

How to make Vegetable Biryani

Step 1 Soak rice for half an hour
Step 2 Add ghee on pan and Fry the spices
Step 3 Fry Sliced onion and Ginger garlic paste
Step 4 Add Garam Masala and saute it
Step 5 Add Vegetables, Rice and Curd
Step 6 Add Water and close the pan, Cook in low flame for 15-20 min
Step 7 Add some ghee and serve hot

Tips

  • You can make it more aromatic by adding saffron strings.
  • You can also add cashews over the biryani for a royal touch.
  • Serve with a gravy of your choice and raita.
